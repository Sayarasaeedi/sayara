Artist Statement

Art has always been my way of expressing feelings and stories. Through my portraits, I aim to capture the depth of human emotion and the unique stories that shape each individual. Using pencil and charcoal, I focus on simplicity, allowing me to convey raw, powerful expressions. Every line, shadow, and detail in my work is intentional, drawing viewers into the emotions that define a person.

My work explores themes of identity, emotions, and the connections between people, shaped by my personal journey and background. I believe that art has the ability to speak universally, and I hope that when people engage with my work, they feel a sense of connection to the individuals I portray and reflect on the emotions behind each face.
